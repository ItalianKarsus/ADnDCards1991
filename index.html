About this Project

I'm Italian Karsus, @italiankarsus on Twitter, trying to find the artist for every card in the 1991 set, and then in the 1992 and 1993 sets, for completionism's sake.

I've split them into three: a master list of everything, All Cards, one that's got only cards I haven't yet determined who drew, Unfound.

Unfound yet Recognizable are those that appear to have been images taken from an existing TSR project. Those may still be recognized by someone familiar with the style.

I've done like 500 of them already, man, I'm tired... if you can help, I beseech you do!

No further ado here'
